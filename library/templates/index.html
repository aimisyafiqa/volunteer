KASUKMA 24 INDERA MAHKOTA

KPM (Kolej Profesional MARA) Indera Mahkota is one of the educational institutions under the management of Majlis Amanah Rakyat (MARA), located in Indera Mahkota, Kuantan, Pahang. KPM focuses on providing diploma-level education and skills training in various professional fields, particularly for Bumiputera students. It aims to prepare graduates for the workforce through a combination of theoretical and practical learning.
